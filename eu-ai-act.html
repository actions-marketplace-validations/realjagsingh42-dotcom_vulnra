EU AI Act Compliance Checklist

The EU AI Act is the world's first comprehensive AI regulation with enforceable penalties. It applies to any AI system deployed in the EU — regardless of where the company building it is located.

Deadline: August 2, 2026. Fines reach €30 million or 6% of global annual turnover. VULNRA generates EU AI Act compliance reports in 60 seconds.

Key Deadlines (2025-2027)

Date Milestone Who is affected
Aug 2, 2025 GPAI Model rules Companies deploying General Purpose AI models in the EU.
Aug 2, 2026 High-risk systems AI used in employment, education, healthcare, and finance.
Aug 2, 2027 Remaining systems All general-purpose AI not in high-risk categories.

Critical Articles for LLMs

  • Article 9: Risk Management — Implement systems to mitigate foreseeable misuse.
  • Article 13: Transparency — Users must know they are interacting with an AI system.
  • Article 15: Robustness — High-risk AI must be resilient to adversarial manipulation.
