India DPDP & AI Compliance: The Complete Guide

India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDP Act 2023) applies to every company processing personal data of Indian citizens — including every SaaS product using an LLM to handle user inputs.

Fines for violations reach ₹250 crore. VULNRA is the only LLM security scanner that automatically tests your AI system against India DPDP requirements.

Key DPDP Sections for AI Companies

Section Title What it means for AI
Section 4 Grounds for Processing LLM features must have a lawful basis (consent or legitimate use).
Section 8 Data Fiduciary Obligations Safeguards must be proportionate to risk. No retention policies violate this.
Section 11 Right to Erasure Users can request deletion of data from vector stores and logs.
Section 16 Cross-Border Transfer Restricts transferring Indian user data to unapproved countries.

Compliance Checklist

  • Map all personal data flows through your LLM system.
  • Implement a data deletion workflow for Section 11 requests.
  • Run a VULNRA scan to test for PII leakage.
